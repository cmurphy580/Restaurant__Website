About

FAICCO'S Restaurant was opened by award winning Chef Luciano Canova to offer residents and visitors of southern Brooklyn with the authentic Italian cuisine that the Faicco's name has become synonymous with after the success of the Faicco's restaurant in Las Vegas. The restaurant offers traditional Roman recipes passed down from Chef Luciano's family, as well as inventive Northern and Southern Italian preparations inspired by Chef Luciano's culinary education and travels.
